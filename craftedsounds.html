Crafted Sounds


On my eighteenth birthday I started an record label.

What began as a primitive effort to create limited-run physical media for independent artists out of my parents' house in 2016 has gradually become a notable label home for alternative acts in the mid-Atlantic region.

Operations have grown over the years as I have tacked on skills in social media, graphic design, web design, PR, and show booking while also learning how to run a small business and distribute music digitally (Spotify, Apple, etc.) and physically (independent record stores). However, the most rewarding part of this has been the relationships that I have along the way that will last a lifetime.

Achievements:

  • Founded the label and released CS001 on my 18th birthday (2016) as a senior in high school.
  • Raised $2,000 for Planned Parenthood of Western Pennsylvania through projects in 2017 and 2019.
  • Launched website in August 2017.
  • Signed to the Misra Records Label Group in February 2018 for distribution through Redeye Worldwide.
  • Attended a festival in Slovakia via label peers Z Tapes while studying abroad in Berlin, Germany
  • Booked and Planned an unofficial SXSW 2020 showcase (was canceled due to COVID-19)
  • Established a sync licensing relationship with Park the Van in January 2021.
  • Crafted Sounds LLC was registered in April 2021.
  • Put out music on vinyl format (label-funded release) and utilized physical distribution (in addition to e-commerce and in-person sales) for the first time in August 2021.
  • Eyebawl performed at Firefly Music Festival in September 2021. - Full Set Recording
  • ​Partnered with The Business for physical distribution in February 2022.
  • ​​Hosted a showcase at Deutschtown Music Festival in July 2022 with acts from DC, PHL, and PGH.
  • ​​Left the Misra Records Label Group and partnered with Symphonic for digital and physical distribution in July 2022.
  • feeble little horse signed with Saddle Creek and went on a week-long tour with Merce Lemon in December 2022.
  • Booked shows for national acts including but not limited to: Jack Stauber, Crumb, feeble little horse, Dummy, Peel Dream Magazine
  • Managed to exist for over eight years and put out over 40 records.
  • Partnered with Polyvinyl Licensing to pitch catalog for TV, film, video game placement in June 2023.
  • Gaadge's album Somewhere Down Below was reviewed by Pitchfork in August 2023.
