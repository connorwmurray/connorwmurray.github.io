Crafted Sounds


On my eighteenth birthday I started a record label.

What began as a primitive effort to create limited-run physical media for independent artists in 2016 has gradually become a home for emerging alternative acts in the mid-Atlantic region.

Operations have grown over the years as I have tacked on skills in social media, graphic design, web design, PR, and show booking while also learning how to run a small business and distribute music digitally (Spotify, Apple, etc.) and physically (independent record stores). However, the most rewarding part of this has been the relationships that I have made along the way that will last a lifetime.
